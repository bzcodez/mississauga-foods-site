Brunch

"Brunch is always a good idea"

The most popular brunch venues in Mississauga are:

  • Breakfast ING
  • Panagio's
  • Captain Robin’s Family Restaurant

Breakfast ING

Breakfast ING is a small, but amazingly cozy Taiwanese/Malaysian inspired breakfast place. Their menu offerings include different Taiwanese, Malaysian, Hong Kong breakfast dishes, crepes and sandwiches, soups and many other meals perfect for a brunch date!

Captain Robin's Family Restaurant

Captain Robin’s Family Restaurant has been serving delicious homemade breakfasts, lunches and dinners for over 4 years. They feature generous portions of breakfasts like the house favourite the Steak Benedict. Stop by for a delicious home style brunch during any part of the day!
